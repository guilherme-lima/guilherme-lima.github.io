    About Me

    Meet my professional trajectory.

    • 2001-2007

      First steps

      My first job was as Informatic Teacher. Later I started my first own business, a Cybercafé. This experience teached me a lot, but I wanted more.

    • 2008-2014

      First Big Company

      Searching for new experiences, I got a job in a big Brazilian company. A trainee position that could reveal on me a great learning capacity. In a few weeks I became a Consultant, working with public management systems. Years later, I decided to study Public Management and Law.

    • 2014-2015

      Winds of Change

      This was a year of great changes in my life. I lived in Europe for a year and a half. Living abroad is an unique experience. It really took me out of my comfort zone and made me stronger. So then I decided that it was time to restart my career, but now as a Software Developer.

    • 2016-Now

      Starting Over

      Back to Brazil, I was approved in an internship program as a Software Developer at Stefanini S/A, one among the 100 biggest IT companies in the world (BBC News). After six months I grabbed a job offer as a Systems Analyst. Mission accomplished! For now, I just know that I love what I do and I feel like I was born to do that, and I wake up everyday willing to learn more and improve my skills.
